# Product Spec

    ## ICP

    biotech founders, research analysts, and diligence teams

    ## Problem

    slow paper triage, unclear translational risk, and scattered experiment notes

    ## Workflow

    paste abstract or study notes, extract claims, score evidence, list risks, and suggest next experiments

    ## Differentiation

    Turns raw user input into a scored, explainable, action-ready workflow instead of a generic chat response.

    ## Premium Features

    - structured intake
- quality scoring
- risk review
- executive summary
- export-ready action plan

    ## Pricing

    $1,500/month diligence workspace

    ## Risk Notes

    Human review is required for regulated or high-stakes decisions.
